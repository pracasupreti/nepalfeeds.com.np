    About Us

    Nepal Feeds and Agrotech Pvt. Ltd. (formerly Shree Belbari Krishak Dana Udhyog Pvt. Ltd.) was founded in 2010 by Mr. Santosh Prasain & Kabindra Prasain. The factory is located in Eastern part of Nepal and has been serving all over the Nepal with 3000 plus strong dealers' network. Nepal Feed is not only the biggest producer in Morang but also has a high quality and rich customers satisfaction.

    Our product has Double Dose quality where any farmer can have optimum results with their prduction.

    Feed plays a greater role to uplift production, farm as well as farmer. Our product impacts all 3 sectors and gives higher benefits with "Double Dose in Single Pack".
